Add a premium iOS-style interaction layer to the Now screen.

Goal:
Make the experience feel like a native iPhone app through subtle haptics, motion, and feedback — calm, minimal, and high-quality.

Do NOT change functionality or layout. Only enhance interaction and feedback.

---

Part 1 — Haptics

Add haptic feedback at key moments only:

1. Arm auto-registration (Get Spot)
- medium impact

2. Cancel auto-registration
- light impact

3. Registration success
- success notification

4. Registration failure
- error notification

5. Optional:
- very subtle selection feedback when checklist reaches fully ready state

Constraints:
- do NOT fire haptics on polling or checklist updates
- do NOT repeat haptics rapidly

---

Part 2 — Button press physics

For primary and secondary buttons:

1. On press down:
- scale to ~0.97
- duration 80–120ms

2. On release:
- return to scale 1.0
- duration 160–220ms
- soft spring easing

3. Visual:
- slight opacity reduction (~0.9)
- no ripple or harsh effects

4. State changes:
- crossfade button labels instead of remounting
- avoid layout shift

---

Part 3 — Countdown digit animation

Enhance countdown updates:

1. Only animate digits that change

2. Animation:
- old digit: fade out + move up ~4px
- new digit: fade in + move from below (~4px → 0)

3. Timing:
- 120–180ms
- ease-out

4. Do NOT animate the entire timer block

---

Part 4 — Subtle sound cues (optional)

Add very minimal sound feedback:

1. Registration success:
- soft confirmation tone

2. Registration failure:
- soft muted alert tone

3. Optional:
- light tap sound when arming auto-registration

Constraints:
- keep volume low
- do not loop or repeat
- do not add sounds to countdown or checklist updates

---

Part 5 — Motion coordination

Ensure animations feel cohesive:

- button transitions: 180–220ms
- checklist transitions: 180–240ms
- text fades: 150–200ms
- countdown digits: 120–180ms
- heartbeat pulse: 1.4–1.8s

Avoid overlapping or competing animations.

---

Part 6 — Composed state transitions

When auto-registration becomes armed:

1. checklist completes
2. slight delay (~150ms)
3. confidence fades in
4. button transitions

This should feel intentional and calm, not instantaneous.

---

Design intent:
- calm
- subtle
- Apple-like
- emotionally reassuring
- no flashy or noisy effects
